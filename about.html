About Smart Interview Preparation Portal

Smart Interview Preparation Portal is a web application designed to help students prepare for placement interviews through coding practice, aptitude tests, technical MCQs, HR interview questions, resume management and performance tracking.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make interview preparation simple, accessible and effective for every student.

Coding Practice

Improve your programming skills.

Aptitude

Practice quantitative and logical reasoning.

HR Interview

Prepare common HR interview questions.

Performance

Track your learning progress.

Our Vision

To become a one-stop platform for interview preparation and help students achieve their career goals.

Our Goal

Build confidence through regular practice and continuous performance improvement.
